A Legacy of Royal Luxury

Founded in 1892, Forever stands as a testament to timeless grandeur and impeccable taste. Nestled amidst manicured gardens and classical architecture, our palace hotel has welcomed royalty, dignitaries, and discerning travellers from across the world.

Every corner of Forever tells a story — from the hand-painted ceilings of the Grand Ballroom to the whisper-soft linens of our bespoke suites. Here, tradition and modernity intertwine to create experiences that linger long after departure.
